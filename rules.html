The Red Box is Your Hand Area. Opponents Can Not See Your Cards in this Area.

Right Click Flips a Card Over. Ctrl + Right Click Taps a Card

Double Left Click Adds Text to Your Card, and the Second Prompt's Response Becomes Viewable While Hovering Over the Card. Ctrl + Double Left Click Sends a Card to the Bottom of Your Library.

Click the Search Button in the Tools Tab to Search the Deck. Move the Mouse Cursor Over Your Deck and use the Left, Right, and Down Arrow Keys to Cycle Thru Cards and Choose Where They Will be put After Searching Finishes (by Clicking the Search Button Again). For Internet Explorer and Edge you must move the mouse between each key press.

To Start a New Game Both Clients Need to be Refreshed and Then Choose the Decks.
